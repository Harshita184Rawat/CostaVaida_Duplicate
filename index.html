A Fresh Spin On Fresh Mex

100% Pronounceable Ingredients

No weird words, no nonsense. Just naturally whole & wholesome, farm-to-fork fresh ingredients (that most two-year-olds can say).

No Food Comas

Every ingredient in our balanced meals is served in perfectly-digestible 2 and 4-ounce portions. That way, you feel good after you eat…not ready for a siesta.

Get Smothered In Love, (Free)

Smothering is the wave (pun intended). Smother your burrito or enchiladas in one of our delicious sauces free of charge!

Every Ingredient Has A Story

Our raw ingredients are carefully curated, because scrupulous sourcing ensures impeccable quality. (In other words, we pretty much “hand pick” everything and buy only the best.)

Flour Power

Our flour tortillas are made with high-quality malted flour (for perfect flavor & texture). And we use palm oil rather than lard (for our vegetarian friends).

Perfect Peppers

Our crisp, clean, lush-green chiles are Mother Nature’s finest. Warm not hot and farm fresh from where they grow best—Hatch, New Mexico.

Steakhouse Steak

Responsibly sourced from the same Midwestern family ranch that 9 of the top 10 US steakhouses go to.

Avocado Bravado

We have to brag about our nutty-creamy avocados from Michoacán, Mexico. Its perfect subtropical climate and long growing season produce phenomenal flavor and texture.
